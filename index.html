About XYaiops

Built for faster vulnerability response

XYaiops is an early-stage software startup focused on helping engineering and security teams respond to vulnerabilities with less manual coordination. We are building practical AI workflows that turn fragmented security findings into prioritized, owner-ready remediation work.

Our team is small, engineering-heavy, and product-focused. We are actively developing the first version of our platform and using AI-assisted development to iterate quickly on core product capabilities.

Company stage Early-stage startup founded in 2025
Team profile Approximately 10 people, mostly focused on coding and product development
Primary focus AI-assisted vulnerability triage, remediation, and verification workflows

Platform vision

One operating layer for the full vulnerability lifecycle

01

Continuous Discovery

Normalize findings from scanners, code, dependencies, tickets, and runtime signals into one response queue.

02

AI Prioritization

Combine severity, exploitability, business context, ownership, and SLA pressure into focused action queues.

03

Guided Remediation

Generate concise remediation plans, suggested owners, fix evidence, and developer tickets that fit existing engineering workflows.

04

Closed-loop Verification

Validate fixes automatically, track SLA progress, and preserve audit-ready proof across the full lifecycle.

Use cases

Designed for teams that need to move from finding to fix

Security Triage

Reduce duplicate findings, highlight exploitable issues, and explain why a vulnerability should be handled now.

Developer Handoff

Create clear action plans with affected component details, ownership clues, remediation guidance, and acceptance criteria.

SLA Tracking

Keep response work visible by severity, owner, due date, and current remediation status.

Fix Verification

Confirm that remediation work is complete and maintain evidence for review, audit, and customer security conversations.
